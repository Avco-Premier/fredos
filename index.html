Our Story

Welcome to Fredo's

Since 1987, Fredo's Pizza & Pasta has been a cornerstone of the Massapequa Park community, serving authentic Italian cuisine with passion and pride.

Our dough is made fresh daily using traditional recipes that have been perfected over decades. We offer a full arrangement of Italian dishes and pizza, prepared with the same care and dedication that has made our family proud for over 35 years.

We deliver, and we also cater for any occasion. We look forward to continuing to serve this wonderful community with good, wholesome Italian food that brings families together.

— The Fredo's Family
